# MindGuard Quick Reference Card

## 🎤 Voice Features

### Voice Chat
1. Open AI Chat page
2. Click "Start Voice Chat"
3. Speak your message
4. Click "Stop Recording"
5. Message auto-sends

### Self-Introduction (3 minutes)
1. Click "🎤 Start Recording"
2. Talk about your challenges, feelings, stress
3. Click "⏹ Stop"
4. Review/edit transcript
5. Click "🔍 Analyze Needs"
6. View your needs analysis

**What to say**: Academic stress, family pressure, loneliness, sleep issues, anxiety, motivation problems, or anything affecting your wellbeing.

**Privacy**: Audio stays in your browser. Only text is analyzed. Visible to your counselor.

### Clear Self-Introduction
- Click "🗑 Clear" button
- Confirm deletion
- Everything resets

## 🎮 Games

Access from Dashboard → Educational Mini Games

1. **Word Search** - Find mental health words (3 min)
2. **Number Puzzle** - Sliding tile challenge (5 min)
3. **Memory Cards** - Match emoji pairs (4 min)

## 📺 Videos

Access from Dashboard → Personalized Videos

Categories match your PFADS assessment:
- Emotional Distress
- Academic Stress
- Social & Peer Support
- Family Stress
- Coping Skills
- General Wellness

## 📱 Install on Phone

### Android
Menu (⋮) → Add to Home screen

### iOS
Share (□↑) → Add to Home Screen

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Voice not working?**
- Allow microphone permission
- Use Chrome or Safari
- Speak clearly

**Transcript empty?**
- Type manually in text box
- Check language setting
- Try again

**Games not loading?**
- Refresh page
- Clear cache
- Check internet

## 🆘 Need Help?

- Contact your counselor via dashboard
- Use AI Chat for immediate support
- Complete daily check-in
- Book counseling session

## 🔒 Your Privacy

✅ Voice recorded locally
✅ Only text sent to server
✅ Encrypted connection
✅ You control your data
✅ Can delete anytime
